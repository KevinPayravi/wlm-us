Wiki Loves Monuments in the United States

Wiki Loves Monuments is an annual international photographic competition that invites people to document and promote historic sites around the world by uploading photos to the Wikimedia Commons to be used on Wikipedia and other Wikimedia projects.

Wiki Loves Monuments is a federated event, with each country hosting their own campaign. This site serves as a simple hub for United States' annual contest pages. Please visit the page for the current year for more info.

To learn more about the global contest, visit the international site.
